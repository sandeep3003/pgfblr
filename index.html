Welcome to Penial Gospel Fellowship

The Almighty God has chosen his humble servant Dr.K.Nireekshan Paul as his instrument for the extension of his kingdom in Eluru (West Godavari District) and other surrounding places nearby. He accepted Jesus as his personal saviour at the age of 16 and he gave up his Job at the age of 21 with god given burden and focus He started Peniel Gospel Fellowhsip in 1997 and God blessed his ministry abundantly. Today nealry 40 places sub centers started and worship happens every Sunday.

The good lord also has shown us his amazing grace to start a branch in Banglore(Marathahalli) through Dr.K.Nireekshan Paul prayer on April 18th 2011. Subsequently Bro Ravi Kumar and Bro Vijay Kumar worked through the grace of God and amazing help by congregation After worked for few years at Marathahalli branch, God also opned doord for English service on June 4th 2017 and Kengeri on 2015. God continuosly helpign us and we prayerfully depend upon him by faith for ever need and on March 7th 2020 , after years of prayer , God also opened doors for us in whote field area.
